About Us

Built by the Community, For the Community

The O'Donnell Resettlement Waterworks Multipurpose Cooperative — ORWAMCO — is a fully registered, member-owned cooperative located in Capas, Tarlac. We were founded on the principle that community members, when organized, can govern their own essential services better than any outside entity.

As a water service provider, we manage the water distribution system ensuring clean and reliable supply to every connected household in the resettlement area. As a multipurpose credit cooperative, we allow members to pool their savings, access low-interest loans, and build collective financial resilience.

Our cooperative is governed democratically by its members — every member has a voice, every vote counts equally.

Our Services

What We Offer

From reliable water distribution to member savings programs, ORWAMCO provides essential services that strengthen the O'Donnell Resettlement community.

💧

Water Distribution

We manage and maintain the community water system, ensuring safe, potable water reaches every connected household efficiently and consistently.

🏦

Savings Program

Members can deposit and grow their savings within the cooperative, earning dividends while contributing to the community's collective financial strength.

📋

Low-Interest Loans

Access to affordable credit for members — for emergencies, livelihood projects, education, or home improvements — with fair and transparent terms.

🔧

System Maintenance

Our technical team regularly inspects, repairs, and upgrades water infrastructure to ensure uninterrupted service and water quality compliance.

📑

Billing & Payments

Transparent, fair monthly billing for water consumption. Members can settle payments conveniently at our office or through authorized channels.

🌱

Livelihood Support

Special financial programs and cooperative initiatives designed to help members develop sustainable income sources and improve their quality of life.

Membership

Join the Cooperative

Membership is open to all residents of the O'Donnell Resettlement area. Joining is simple, and the benefits last a lifetime.

01

Attend an Orientation

Visit the ORWAMCO office to attend a free membership orientation and learn about your rights and responsibilities as a cooperative member.

02

Submit Requirements

Provide a valid government-issued ID, proof of residency in the O'Donnell Resettlement area, and a completed membership application form.

03

Pay Initial Share Capital

Make the initial share capital contribution — a one-time payment that entitles you to full membership and a stake in the cooperative.

04

Welcome to ORWAMCO

Receive your membership certificate, passbook, and begin enjoying all services, voting rights, and benefits as a full cooperative member.

Member Benefits

  • Access to safe and reliable water supply at regulated rates
  • Low-interest loans for personal, livelihood, and emergency needs
  • Annual dividend based on share capital and patronage
  • Right to vote and run for the Board of Directors
  • Access to cooperative savings and time-deposit programs
  • Participation in General Assembly and community decisions
  • Financial education and cooperative development programs
